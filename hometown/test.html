Visit Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital and most populous city of Japan, officially known as the Tokyo Metropolis. It is located on the central coast of Honshu island, at the head of Tokyo Bay, within the Kanto Plain. While often referred to as a city, Tokyo functions more like a prefecture, comprising 23 special wards, 26 cities, and several towns and villages, including remote island chains like the Izu and Ogasawara Islands.

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Tokyo.
